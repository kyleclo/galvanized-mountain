Proclamation (Public Copy)

Citizens!

Albert has fallen! As in down a cliff. Onto a metal barge. Which was
on fire. And surrounded by sharks. In an acid lake. That were
genetically mutated to be able to fly. Specifically in poison gas.

daggers. That are pointed up at the cliff. Which has a thousand
carnivorous giraffes. Armed with krises.
